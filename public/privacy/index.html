Our lawful bases for the collection and use of your data Our lawful bases for collecting or using personal information to provide and improve products and services for clients are:

All of your data protection rights may apply except the right to object. • Legitimate interests – we’re collecting or using your information because it benefits you, our organisation or someone else, without causing an undue risk of harm to anyone. All of your data protection rights may apply, except the right to portability.
